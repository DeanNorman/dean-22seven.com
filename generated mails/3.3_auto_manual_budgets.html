A budget made for you, or by you.
22seven creates a personalised budget for you, automatically, based on your own actual behaviour. It calculates your average spending on each category over the last few months, and shows you how you’re doing against that so far this month.

You can also create your own budget. Instead of tracking your spending against your average, you can set your own target amount for any category. (Here’s how to do it on web, Android or iPhone.)
